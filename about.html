Kevin McCaffrey is a Senior Reporter at Haymarket Media and writes for MM&M. His work primarily concerns marketing, advertising, how drug companies sell their medicines, and clinical research and development. He has extensive experience writing news articles, features, and long-form explainers. His writing has also appeared in PRWeek, Campaign, among others, and has been cited by The Wall Street Journal, The Boston Globe, as well as other blogs and trade outlets. He graduated from Rutgers University in May 2011. Other interests include web development with a focus in JavaScript. He can be reached at kevinericmccaffrey [at] gmail [dot] com.
